Local Impact: Partnering with Sunrise of Issaquah

After our incredible journey serving 392 people in India, UHAF is officially bringing that same mission home. We are excited to announce our first local partnership with the Sunrise of Issaquah Senior Center!

Our goal for this weekend is simple: be the extra hands and friendly faces that the residents and staff need. We are looking for volunteers to join us and act as personal assistants—helping with anything from technical support on devices to participating in afternoon activities and sharing stories.

Event Details & Recruitment

  • Date: Saturday, May 30, 2026
  • Time: 1:00 PM – 5:00 PM (Come anytime in between!)
  • Location: Sunrise of Issaquah Senior Center
Email Us to Join DM on Instagram

We need volunteers ASAP to finalize our coordination with the center. This is a meaningful way to gain service hours while directly improving the lives of seniors in our community. Whether you're helping someone send a photo to their grandkids or just sharing a conversation, your time makes a massive difference.

Contact us at uhaf.nonprofit@gmail.com to get involved.
