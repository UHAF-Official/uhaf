Support Our Cause

In 2024, I raised funds for two free medical camps for the underprivileged in remote and rural areas of Western Rajasthan, India. I was there in December 2024 and led the camps in-person. I have posted pictures and stories of people who benefited from this initiative. You can read more about this initiative HERE.

My goal was to collect $900 to cover the cost of transportation and snacks for a team of doctors and their support staff, medicines, and spreading awareness about the camps.

Fundraising Status (2024 Camps)

We raised a total of $510! We were $390 away from our goal, but still succeeded in getting medical care to 392 patients for free!
